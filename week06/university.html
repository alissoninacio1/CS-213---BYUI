About Us

Founded in the golden age since the Northern Kingdom split from the other kingdoms, Valerianno Institute of Technology has offered the highest academic level and the highest standard of study in the six kingdoms. Lord Blackwood decided to start this respected institution with the crown in the 4th year of King Benjaminn Kreiggorf's reign.

Admissions

Applications are made three times a year and all documents must be sent by letter. The average required to enter university is 8.5 points. We are on Western Rock, south path, near the black forest, at number 77A, Archeland - Troyrope. Exams involve logic, physical strength, athletic prowess, survival and natural sciences.
